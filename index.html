About Us

ELITE ROTTU KADAI is A Pure Heaven for Non-Veg lovers, you will get best delicious and freshly prepared chicken and meat dishes. Our cuisine consists of South India, Chinese, Arabian and Tandoori. Our special dishes are a varieties of VAAZHAILLAI PAROTTA, GRILL, BBQ and TANDOORI CHICKEN. Our rottukadai special KARI DOSAI and BUN PAROTTA are a must try ones.
